Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Tomatoe paste
  • Beef broth
  • Canned mushrooms
  • A splash of wine
  • Onion, celery and fresh garlic
  • Dried whole cloves

Now..the steps

  1. Brown the beef, then throw in chopped veggues to simmer.
  2. Dump the pasta sauce ingredients into pot for a 10 min boil.
  3. Cook your noodles.
  4. Combine and enjoy!
